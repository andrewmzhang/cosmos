Parity

1. Your computer is going to transmit these bytes with even parity. What value of parity bit should your computer send after each byte to ensure that each byte and its associated parity bit has even parity?

2. Your computer has received the following bytes, each with an associated parity bit. Each of the parity bits originally was sent as even parity. Based on the values of the bytes and the parity bits, you can be certain that some of the bytes have been received incorrectly. please select them.

3. Your computer has received the following byte 00001001 along with its even parity bit 0. Based on their values, your colleague claims that the byte has been received correctly. Do you agree? Why?
